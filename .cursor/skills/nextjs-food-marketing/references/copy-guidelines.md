# Copy Guidelines

Voice:

* Professional
* Modern
* Confident

Focus:

* Revenue growth
* Direct orders
* Customer retention
* Brand ownership

Preferred wording:

* Increase direct orders
* Keep more revenue
* Own your customer relationships
* Grow repeat business
* Build customer loyalty

Avoid:

* Synergy
* Revolutionary
* Cutting-edge
* Disruptive
* World-class
* Industry-leading

Keep headlines under 10 words when possible.

Keep paragraphs under 3 lines.
